Tips for Propcott Creators

Set and simplify

Remember that when you start a propcott, you set the terms for how others can take part. Try to make your propcott as simple as possible so people can understand and take action with ease.

Share

The power of social media is no secret. Share your propcott page on all social networks available to you and encourage your friends/followers to spread the word.

Monitor and Keep track

After you publish your propcott, utilize our variety of tools to monitor and keep track of your propcott.

Communicate

Chat and engage in dialogue with your supporters by posting updates and by commenting through the Facebook plugin.

Do's

  • Explain HOW to boycott with specific simple words and phrases
  • Put yourself in supporters shoes. Would this be an easy task to undertake?

Dont's

  • Don't simply say "boycott" in the "How" section. Explain how to. For example, in order to boycott a food and beverage conglomerate, list out the company's 3 best selling products for others to avoid.
  • Don't lie. Be honest with your information, sources, and most importantly, your purpose.
