You've joined a Propcott.
Now what?

Joining a Propcott was the first step-pledging to take action. As a participant in a propcotting campaign, it's your job now to follow through and take action. Here are a couple of tips we've came up with to help you.

Trust

Trust your fellow propcotters will take action alongside you for the duration of the campaign.
Trust that Propcott.com will work tirelessly to help all Propcott campaigns achieve their goal.

Discipline

Combining to abstain from something is not always easy. But just remember why you're doing it and what you're doing it for. Keep the end goal in mind. If you want periodical reminders, edit your notification settings.

Patience

Change doesn't always happen overnight. But with patience and persistence, you can make a difference.

Broadcast

Spread the word! Inform friends and family about the campaign and take action together along with your fellow propcotters.
